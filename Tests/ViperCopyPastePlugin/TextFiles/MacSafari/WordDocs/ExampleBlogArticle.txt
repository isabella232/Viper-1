<p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">Mastering marketing

<p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">5 teething problems of
marketing automation<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial"><o:p>&nbsp;</o:p></span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial"><span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;</span>[Intro]<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">Marketing
automation brings with it many advantages, but the technology is not without
its challenges. Here, we look at 5 aspects of marketing automation that can
take some getting used to. But once you do, there’s no telling where your
marketing efforts will lead! <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;</span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">[Body]<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">The
benefits of marketing automation are well-documented. It:<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpFirst" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:

</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">automates processes.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">·<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">saves time.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">·<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">enables you to better <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">personalise&lt;link
to personalisation blog&gt;</b> your leads.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">·<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">translates to more conversions (as a result of these more optimised
leads).<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">·<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">allows you to measure and report on everything.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpMiddle" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">·<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">creates a central hub for monitoring all of your marketing activities.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraphCxSpLast" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l0 level1 lfo1"><!--[if !supportLists]--><span style="font-family:Symbol;mso-fareast-font-family:Symbol;mso-bidi-font-family:
Symbol"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">·<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;
</span></span></span><!--[endif]--><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">But
reaping these rewards means understanding, adjusting to, and accepting some of
the tricky fundamentals of marketing automation. <o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l1 level1 lfo2"><!--[if !supportLists]--><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-fareast-font-family:
Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">1.<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span></b><!--[endif]--><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">It’s hungry.<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">You
need to feed your ‘marketing-automation machine’ with a steady stream of
content. What’s more, you need to make sure this content is not only regular
but also regularly refreshed. And it must target different customers at <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal">different stages of the sales funnel&lt;will
link to other still-to-be-written-this-week marketing-automation blog&gt;</b>. Alas,
producing this content requires a lot of work, but it ultimately pays big
dividends. High-quality content marketing engages and informs customers and
potential customers and can position your organisation as a true market leader.
<b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><o:p></o:p></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l1 level1 lfo2"><!--[if !supportLists]--><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-fareast-font-family:
Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">2.<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span></b><!--[endif]--><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">It needs a <i style="mso-bidi-font-style:normal">healthy</i> diet. <o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">This
means you and your team need to feed your marketing-automation system properly,
so that it’s regurgitating what you need it to be telling you. If the quality
of your data or processes is lacking, you won’t accurately know when you have a
genuine opportunity to move a prospect through the sales funnel or to convert a
customer. This is true of your lead scoring as well. If the system is not
properly recording data, your scoring will be faulty, and therefore your
customers – at varying stages of the buying journey – will not receive the
correct messaging, and you’ll just be wasting time, effort, and money.<span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp; </span><o:p></o:p></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l1 level1 lfo2"><!--[if !supportLists]--><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-fareast-font-family:
Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">3.<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span></b><!--[endif]--><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">It needs you to keep up with it. <o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">Marketing
automation is new to many and fast-paced for all. To stay up to speed, you may
need to embrace a new mindset and learn new skills, such as the above-mentioned
content marketing and other technologies, such as content-management systems
(CMSs) and customer-relationship-management (CRM) systems. <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:
normal"><o:p></o:p></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l1 level1 lfo2"><!--[if !supportLists]--><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-fareast-font-family:
Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">4.<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span></b><!--[endif]--><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">It costs (continuous) money.<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">Marketing
automation does come with an ongoing cost – it need not necessarily be
expensive, but it <i style="mso-bidi-font-style:normal">does</i> need to be
consistent. If you stop paying for marketing automation, you’ll likely lose any
progress you’ve made and momentum you’ve built up. Again, think of the time,
effort, and money wasted. <b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><o:p></o:p></b></span></p>

<p class="MsoListParagraph" style="text-indent:-18.0pt;mso-list:l1 level1 lfo2"><!--[if !supportLists]--><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-fareast-font-family:
Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial"><span style="mso-list:Ignore">5.<span style="font:7.0pt &quot;Times New Roman&quot;">&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp; </span></span></span></b><!--[endif]--><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:
Arial">It’s daunting!<o:p></o:p></span></b></p>

<p class="MsoNormal"><span style="font-family:Arial;mso-bidi-font-family:Arial">All
this data you’re collecting can be overwhelming. If you’re running a range of
campaigns, the information is often convoluted, and there’s heaps of it! <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;</span>Even when your marketing automation software
is operating smoothly, it’s a lot to manage. It’s important for you and your
team to maintain thorough and ongoing documentation to track what you’re doing,
so that your marketing endeavours can be consistent and seamless no matter who
is working with your marketing-automation system. <span style="mso-spacerun:yes">&nbsp;</span><b style="mso-bidi-font-weight:normal"><o:p></o:p></b></span></p>
