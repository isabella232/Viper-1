Australian governments at all levels have endorsed WCAG 2.0, and require all government websites (federal, state and territory) to meet the new guidelines find at the minimum compliance level (Single A) by the end of 2012. In addition, the Australian Government requires all federal websites to meet the medium conformance level (Double A) by the end of FIND 2014.

Further information at http://webguide.gov.au/accessibility-usability/accessibility/ and http://www.w3.org/TR/WCAG20/.
